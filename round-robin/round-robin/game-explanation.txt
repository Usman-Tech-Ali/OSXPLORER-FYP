### **Game Title: Court Kings (Round Robin Scheduling)**

**The Core Concept:**
This is a **side-scrolling management game**. We visualize the **Round Robin Algorithm** as a basketball shooting drill. The "Time Quantum" is the number of balls in the rack. If a player runs out of balls before finishing their target score, they must go to the back of the line.

---

### **1. The Metaphor (How to Code It)**

* **The CPU = The Hoop.** (Single resource, only one person can use it at a time).
* **The Process = The Player.**
* **Burst Time = Target Score.** (e.g., Rookie needs 3 baskets to finish).


* **Time Quantum = The Ball Rack.** (e.g., Rack holds 3 balls. You get 3 shots per turn).
* **Context Switch = Rotation.** (The time it takes for Player A to leave and Player B to step up).

---

### **2. Asset Usage & Game Flow**

Here is the step-by-step loop your team needs to implement:

1. **Spawn (Arrival):**
* Players (Rookie/Pro/MVP) spawn on the **Left** side of the screen.
* They line up in the **Queue** (The Gym Background).


2. **Dispatch (Context Switch):**
* The first player in line runs to the **Hoop** (Asset 2) on the Right.
* *Animation:* `Run Right` sprite.


3. **Execution (Processing):**
* The player shoots the ball.
* **Logic:** `Ball Count - 1` AND `Target Score - 1`.
* *Animation:* `Shoot` sprite + `Ball` sprite flying.


4. **Quantum Check (The Rule):**
* **Condition A (Process Finished):** If `Target Score == 0`, the player cheers and runs off-screen (Task Complete).
* **Condition B (Quantum Expired):** If `Ball Rack == 0` but `Target Score > 0`...
* **Preemption:** The Coach blows the **Whistle** (Asset 7).
* The player runs back to the **Left** side (behind the queue).
* *Animation:* `Run Left` sprite.





---

### **3. Level Mechanics**

* **Level 1: The Basics (Fixed Quantum)**
* **Quantum:** 3 Balls.
* **Logic:** Pure Round Robin. Rookie (Needs 3) finishes in one turn. MVP (Needs 9) must rotate 3 times.
* *Goal:* Clear the queue.


* **Level 2: The Fumble (Context Switch Overhead)**
* **Mechanic:** We introduce a **Delay**.
* When players switch places, show the **Sweat Drop** (Asset 7) and pause for **1.5 seconds**.
* *Lesson:* If the Quantum is too short, the player spends all their time "Fumbling" (Switching) and no time shooting.


* **Level 3: The Tryouts (Arrival Time & Overflow)**
* **Mechanic:** New players spawn continuously on the Left.
* **Fail Condition:** If the line gets too long (goes off the left side of the screen), it's Game Over.
* *Strategy:* You must balance clearing short tasks (Rookies) to make space in the gym.



### **Summary for the Devs**

* **View:** 2D Side View.
* **Movement:** Left  Right.
* **Key Variable:** `BallsInRack` (resets every turn).
* **Winning:** All players reach `Score == 0`.

This setup covers the entire academic requirement for Round Robin (Cyclic execution, Time Quantums, Context Switching) in a fun, visual loop.
